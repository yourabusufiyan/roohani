== Description ==

Roohani brings your site to life with header and immersive featured images. With a focus on business sites, it features multiple sections on the front page as well as widgets, navigation and social menus, a logo, and more.
Journalists, writers and authors can use it to publish news through blogging or writing with our minimalistic multipurpose magazine / newspaper / blog theme. Bloggers can use ads like adsense and affiliate marketing for your blog page about books, actors or other entertainment as well as write a journal / biography about your personal life with our typography optimised, reading friendly, flat and light design. Personalize its asymmetrical grid with a custom color scheme and showcase your multimedia content with post formats. Our default theme for 2018 works great for any abilities, and on any device.

For more information about Roohani please go to http://www.abusufiyan.com/themes/roohani


== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type Roohani in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.
3. Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.
4. Go to https://codex.wordpress.org/Twenty_Seventeen for a guide on how to customize this theme.
5. Navigate to Appearance > Customize in your admin panel and customize to taste.
